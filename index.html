WHAT IS LISTENDENTON?

We are Denton citizens just like you looking for ways to improve our town in both little ways and big ways. A competitive grant from the Bloomberg Foundation has become available for cities who come up with the best ideas using creative thinking, technology, and public data. We are volunteers asking the question: What problems need fixing in our town?

THIS IS WHERE YOU COME IN...

We want to hear from you. We can address the issues we face as a community only by engaging the citizens of Denton - YOU! Tell us what the city of Denton can do better by answering the questions below. We will evaluate all proposals and select one to present.
